Contacting Health Services in Namibia

If you need to reach health services in Namibia, please follow these steps:

  1. Identify the nearest health facility by visiting the Ministry of Health and Social Services website or using a map application.
  2. Gather necessary information, including your health insurance details if applicable.
  3. Contact the facility directly via phone or visit during working hours to inquire about services, appointment availability, or specific health concerns.
