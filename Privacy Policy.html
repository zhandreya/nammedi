Summary of Privacy Policy for the Health Sector in Namibia

Your personal health information is protected under Namibian law. Health service providers must ensure confidentiality, only sharing your information with consent or legal obligation. You have the right to access your medical records and request corrections.

We collect data to provide quality health services, and this data may include personal details, medical history, and treatment information. We use security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.
